> When is my subscription activated?

The subscription plans are activated within 24 hours of your payment. You'll receive a confirmation email once it is activated.

> When is my subscription getting renewed?

The subscription plans renew automatically every 30 days.

> How to cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time at info@cyrcl.eu without additional charges.

> What happens if my ride durations exceeds the free minutes limit?

Standard rates apply whenever the free minutes of a particular rental session are exceeded.
