About us

Born in the vibrant streets of Sofia, Cyrcl aims to change the way we move, connect, and live in our cities.

•
We are a startup whose young founders decided to return back to Bulgaria - preferred building a brighter future for their home country rather than living in the comfort of a brighter present abroad.

•
At the heart of Cyrcl is a vision of a cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable urban life. We believe that change starts with a choice and we aim to make that choice convenient for you.

•
Our fleet of user-friendly bikes, both traditional and electric, invites everyone to be part of this change. Whether you're commuting, exploring, or just enjoying a leisurely ride, Cyrcl provides an affordable and flexible way to navigate the city while reducing your carbon footprint.

•
We're not just about getting you from point A to B, though. We're about creating a community that values active living, environmental responsibility, and the joy of being in motion. Our mission extends beyond the streets; it's about fostering a culture that prioritizes sustainability and health.

•
As we grow, our commitment remains the same: to provide a service that's more than just convenient – it's impactful. Join us on this journey to a cleaner, greener future, one ride at a time. Together, let's accelerate towards a better tomorrow with Cyrcl.
