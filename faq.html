Getting started

>What is Cyrcl?

Cyrcl is the first Bulgarian service offering short-term rental of mechanical and electric bicycles, otherwise known as bike-sharing.

>How do I pronounce ‚ÄúCyrcl‚Äù and what does it mean?

Our name is a blend between two interrelated words and the symbolism behind them. For us the meaning of circle (this is how we pronounce our name) is intertwined with that of sustainability, while cycle symbolizes progress and continuous motion ‚Äî notions, which we strongly relate to.

>What are the working hours of the service?

Our service is available 24/7 all year round. You can rent the Cyrcl bikes whenever you want to.

Download the mobile app by following this link and enjoy your freedom!

>How to register?

You can register for free through our mobile app by following this link.

>How does it work?

Download the app for free through this link.

  1. Find a nearby bike on the map and reserve it for up to 15 minutes free of charge. Make your choice between a mechanical (marked in orange) or an electric (marked in green) bike.

  2. Scan the QR code to unlock your vehicle, or alternatively type in the 4-digit ID code, visible on the right and left sides of each bike‚Äôs frame.

  3. Enjoy your ride!

  4. Park within the zone outlined in orange on the map.

    For manual bikes, please lock manually using the smart lock above the rear tire, then end your rental from the app by pressing the ‚ÄúLock‚Äù, then ‚ÄúEnd rental session‚Äù buttons on the app.

    E-bikes can lock automatically, so no manual locking is required when ending your session or pausing through the app!

*Use the ‚ÄúPause‚Äù function after locking for quick stops along your ride. You can pause anywhere, including outside the pick-up and drop off zone, if needed. For safety reasons, mechanical bikes require manual locking to enter ‚ÄúPause‚Äù mode.

Need more information? Have a look here or give us a call on +359 896 755 556 or e-mail us at info@cyrcl.eu. Our support team is here for you every day between 8:00 and 24:00h.

>What documents are required?

No ID documentation is required, unless you would like to apply for a specific Pass to get extra benefits. In that case we might request verification, according to the terms of the respective offer.

Please note that the payments for your rides are processed through our online system, so you are required to provide a valid bank card after registering, and before beginning your very first Cyrcl ride.

>Are there any age limits to use the service?

In the interest of safety, the Cyrcl service is only available to people over the age of 16.

>What operating system is required?

Our mobile app is available for Android and iOS. You can download it by following this link.

>Can my child use a Cyrcl bike?

In the interest of safety the Cyrcl service is only available to persons over the age of 16. If you would like to ride together with a family member, please make sure they are also a registered and verified user of the Cyrcl app.

Currently, you can only rent one bike at a time through your user account, however, the same payment method/bank card can be used by more than one account.

>Can I rent two or more bikes from the same account?

If you would like to ride together with friends or family, please make sure they are also registered and verified users of the Cyrcl app.

Currently, you can only rent one bike at a time through your user account, however, the same payment method/bank card can be used by more than one account.

Account and Payments

>How do I pay?

After downloading our mobile app (you can do so here) add your bank card details from the ‚ÄúPayment method‚Äù section on the menu in the top left corner of the screen.

Our system requires you to enter a valid payment method before beginning your first ride. Once added, your card will remain linked to your account for future rides and payments.

>What are the prices?

Our current pricing model is composed of three elements.

For mechanical bikes it is 1.5 BGN unlock fee and 0.15 BGN per minute thereafter. The 24-hour rate is 24 BGN and it applies automatically once that amount is reached.

For electric bikes the pricing is composed of 1.9 BGN unlock fee and 0.29 BGN per minute thereafter. The 24-hour rate is 48 BGN and again it is applied automatically.

You can have a look at our current pricing list and monthly subscriptions here.

>Do I get a discount for a longer rental session?

Yes, the highest fee you can pay for 24 hours is 24 BGN for a mechanical bike, and 48 BGN for an e-bike.

Please keep in mind that the full-day rental fee is valid only if you use the same bike for the entire period without ending your session. If you end your rental session and start a new one, regardless of whether you rent the same bike or a new one, the countdown to 24 / 48 BGN will start again.

Additionally, you can take advantage of our Cyrcl subscriptions, designed for avid urban cyclists and sustainable commuters.

More information about our prices can be found on our website here.

More about the bikes

>What is the maximum speed of the electric bikes?

The Cyrcl e-bikes can reach a maximum speed of 25 km/h, as determined by EU regulations and local traffic law. Please take extra caution with adverse weather and traffic conditions at all times while riding - stay safe!

>What is the maximum range of the electric bikes?

The Cyrcl e-bikes can travel up to 60 km without having to recharge the battery. If you would like to travel further, or need to recharge during your ride, please contact us at +359 896 755 556 for assistance.

>How do I activate the electric motor on the e-bike?

Activation of the electric motor is automatic - all you need to do is push the pedals normally, as you would when riding a mechanical bike, the rest is all done for you!

In case the electric motor assistance does not activate automatically, or if one of the indicators on the bike‚Äôs display illuminates in red - please contact us immediately at +359 896 755 556 for assistance. You will not be charged for any downtime while awaiting assistance from our team.

>How do I adjust the seat of the bike?

Mechanical bikes

  1. Adjust the height and direction of the seat of your mechanical Cyrcl bike by using the small metal lever located under the seat.

  2. Pulling the lever away from the seat allows adjustment.

  3. To fasten at the desired height / direction, push the lever back and lock it in place.


E-bikes

  1. E-bikes are equipped with a similar (yellow coloured) lever directly under the seat.

  2. Please push the lever softly and make sure to apply all your weight on the seat itself in order to lower it.

  3. When released from the lever, the seat of the e-bike automatically climbs up to its standard height.
For further assistance or in case of malfunction, please contact us on +359 896 755 556. Our support team is available every day between 8:00 and 24:00h. You will not be charged for any downtime while awaiting assistance from our team.

>What is the maximum weight supported by the bikes?

The Cyrcl bikes can support weight of up to 120kg.

Please note that the bikes‚Äô baskets are suited to carry items weighing no more than 3kg (for mechanical bikes) and 5kg for e-bikes. Always take caution when carrying items in the basket, and make sure your items are secured before beginning our ride.

>Is using the phone holder on the e-bike safe?

You can use your phone for navigation while riding one of our e-bikes by placing it in the holder on the handlebar. Please make sure that your phone can fit in the holder, and is properly secured before you start your ride. Once you unlock the e-bike and begin your ride, the holder will also charge your smartphone, as long as it supports wireless charging and is properly placed.

*Please keep in mind that the phone holder may not be suitable for all phones and should be used at your own risk.
