Cyrcl adds 100 electric bicycles to its fleet in Sofia

Capital, October 31, 2023

Cyrcl, the only bicycle-sharing mobility company in Sofia, has added 100 electric bikes to its fleet. At the beginning of this summer, the company had 150 bikes , but in the months since, another 100 have been added. With these and the new e-bikes, its fleet in the capital increases to 350. At the start of its activities in April 2022, it consisted of barely 50.
